Description about the parameters

Parameters:
1.Age
2.Gender
3.self_employed: Are you self-employed?
4.family_history: Do you have a family history of mental illness?
5.work_interfere: If you have a mental health condition, do you feel that it interferes with your work?
6.benefits: Does your employer provide mental health benefits?
7.care_options: Do you know the options for mental health care your employer provides?
8.wellness_program : Has your employer ever discussed mental health as part of an employee wellness program?
9.seek_help: Does your employer provide resources to learn more about mental health issues and how to seek help?
10.anonymity:Is your anonymity protected if you choose to take advantage of mental health or substance abuse treatment resources?
11.leave: How easy is it for you to take medical leave for a mental health condition?
12.mental_health_consequence: Do you think that discussing a mental health issue with your employer would have negative consequences?
13.phys_health_consequence: Do you think that discussing a physical health issue with your employer would have negative consequences?
14.coworkers:Would you be willing to discuss a mental health issue with your coworkers?
15.supervisor: Would you be willing to discuss a mental health issue with your direct supervisor(s)?
16.mental_health_interview:Would you bring up a mental health issue with a potential employer in an interview?
17.phys_health_interview: Would you bring up a physical health issue with a potential employer in an interview?
18.mental_vs_physical:Do you feel that your employer takes mental health as seriously as physical health?
19.obs_consequence:Have you heard of or observed negative consequences for coworkers with mental health conditions in your workplace?
20.age_range
